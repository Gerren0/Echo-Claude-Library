## 3. Shipping Modes (Shippers)

| Mode | Definition / Distinguishing Facts |
|---|---|
| **Full Truckload (FTL)** | Dedicated trailer capacity; covers dry van, temp-controlled, flatbed, over-dimensional; cross-border capable |
| **Less Than Truckload (LTL)** | Shared-trailer shipping for smaller freight; standard, volume, expedited, and temperature-sensitive variants |
| **Temperature-Controlled LTL** | Delivered via Roadtex; targets 55–65°F range; food/pharma/confectionery focus; cross-sells EchoChill (33–40°F consolidation) |
| **Partial Truckload (PTL)** | For freight too large for LTL but too small for FTL; "load-to-ride" option (same truck picks up and delivers) |
| **Intermodal Rail** | Combines rail + drayage; Echo is a certified Intermodal Marketing Company (IMC); container types: 53', 40' (standard/high-cube), refrigerated |
| **International Air & Ocean** | FCL/LCL ocean freight, air freight, air charter |
| **Parcel** | Management/audit/optimization service rather than direct carrier capacity (see EchoParcel / Distribution & Fulfillment above) |
| **Specialized** | Expedited (ground/air), heavy haul/over-dimensional, white-glove final mile, multi-store rollout/tear-down |

## 4. Freight Brokerage — Specialized Services

| Service | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Mexico Cross-Border** | Customs brokerage, warehousing, crossdocking, transloading for U.S.–Mexico freight; local presence in Mexico City, Monterrey, Laredo; CTPAT-certified |
| **Canada Cross-Border** | Same category of service for U.S.–Canada freight; USMCA compliance guidance; CTPAT-compliant carriers |
| **Retail Consolidation** | Consolidates multiple suppliers shipping to the same retailer onto one load; aligns RAD/MABD delivery windows to improve OTIF (on-time-in-full) compliance |
| **Refrigerated Consolidation (EchoChill)** | See Section 2 |
| **Port Drayage** | Port-to-rail and port-to-warehouse drayage; unbundled rate structure to avoid per diem/demurrage fees |
| **Drop Trailer Solutions** | Trailer left at a facility for asynchronous pickup; backed by a dedicated trailer pool (partnership with Wabash, a trailer manufacturer) |
| **Small Business Shippers** | Not a distinct freight service — a landing page bundling EchoShip/EchoShip Rewards/EchoInsure+/Instant Quotes for smaller shipper accounts |

## 5. Warehousing (Roadtex-enabled)

- FDA-registered, temperature-controlled and dry warehousing facilities.
- Web-based Warehouse Management System (WMS) with real-time inventory visibility.
- Services: order selection, container unloading, cross-dock, display building, kitting, repacking, shrink packaging, overwrapping, UPC/standard labeling.
- **Display Building**: conveyor-line production of retail pallet displays (half/quarter-pallet displays, floor stands, variety packs).
- **Direct-to-Consumer**: e-commerce fulfillment with real-time tracking, expedited label creation, cold packing, and marketplace integrations (Amazon, Walmart.com).
- Industry specializations: confectionery, nutraceutical, retail/mass-merchant, pharmaceutical, food & grocery.

## 7. Managed Transportation (Service Line)

Outsourced transportation management for contracted shippers. Structural components:
- Strategic supply chain solutions (process mapping, network strategy, benchmarking).
- Dedicated account management team.
- TMS technology (order-level shipment optimization, real-time tracking, vendor portal).
- Carrier management (RFP sourcing, compliance monitoring, KPI-driven).
- Carrier invoice auditing (100% of invoices audited).
- Standardized/custom reporting and BI dashboards.
- Uses EchoTrak (rather than EchoShip) as the shipper-facing portal.
